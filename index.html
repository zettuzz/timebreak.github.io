About Us

Welcome to Breaktime, your go-to resource for budget-friendly cafes in San Jose, Occidental Mindoro! Our mission is simple: to guide students to the best cafes in town, offering affordable options without breaking the bank. Whether you need a quiet spot to study or a vibrant atmosphere to unwind, we've got you covered. Join us as we explore the rich coffee culture of San Jose, one cafe at a time. Let's discover new flavors, create lasting memories, and support local businesses together!
